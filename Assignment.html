Introduction


People are fasinated with fish as it's a simple pet to keep. It's a family friendly option and would be a great pet for
people who have stress, high blood pressure and heart rate. Keeping a fish as a pet can reduce stress, reduce blood
pressure and heart rate, and improve behaviour and eating habits of those who suffer with Alzheimer's


An average life span for aquarium fish is 2 years, and aqurium fish can live upto 14 years if you take a special care.



Health and Care


But Keeping a fish may turn out challenging, without adequate place, a fish might not thrive.
To prevent your fish from dying, you will need to give a special care to it.
Take these sentences as the better place for your little fish.
Provide a sufficient place for your fish to thrive.
Feed your fish with a balanced diet
Clean the tank to prevent any infection on your fish
Provide a sufficient water with oxgyen for your fish
Select a good species of fish or make sure that your fishes are not fighting, (research species of fish that you and your family perfers)

Types of infection and dieseases on fish

Just like us, humans and animals, fishes get an infection too, mainly due to inadequate living space, stress or uncleaned tank.


INFECTION NAME WHAT IT DOES
Fin rot col It destroys the fins and tails of the fish, and once it reached the fin base, the fish may die.
Mouth fungus on fish It produces a fuzzy cottonlike growth bacteria, which hinders breathing and eats away the jaws.
Dropsy The belly fills with fluids and becomes swollen, internal organs are damaged, and ultimately the fish will die 13 year old
